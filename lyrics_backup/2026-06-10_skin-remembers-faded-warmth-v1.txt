[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the gentle pull of hands across my chest
A sweet and slow surrender, laying me to rest
You’re staring at my lips beneath the fading light
I want to lose my way inside your arms tonight

[Verse 1]
The gravel road is quiet and the engine’s cold
I’m holding onto secrets that are left untold
Your breath against my collar sends a sudden thrill
The shadows pulling closer on the windowsill

[Pre-Chorus]
There’s a heavy kind of tension in the open air
I run my trembling fingers through your tangled hair
We’re leaning past the edge of everything we know
A sweet and steady rhythm starting to unfold

[Chorus]
'Cause my skin remembers faded warmth
We’re holding onto shelter in a heavy storm
I’m melting in the motion of your steady breath
A beautiful collision tearing through my chest

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] (Faded warmth, faded warmth)
My body’s still holding onto your faded warmth

[Verse 2]
A lonely cup of coffee cooling in the mug
I’m pulling you beside me on this woven rug
I hear the wooden floorboards creaking down the hall
As I pin your shadow gently to the painted wall

[Bridge]
The shadows fall together, turning into one
A quiet kind of aching under the setting sun
The rhythm keeps repeating, running through my head
Even Saeed is captive to the things you said

[Spoken]
I trace the empty spaces where you used to lay
Just wishing I could hold you till the break of day
